About the Brand

Gloves Warrior is more than a brand – it’s a movement. It started as an idea and quickly inspired its own founder, Pangaa Ironside Uaseuapuani, to step into the ring. Since 2024, Gloves Warrior has reached pro boxers like Charles Shinima, Special One Nangolo, Herry Simon Jr, and Nata Heita, and local training communities including Greysblock, Ministry of Gender's youth center, and more.

Our Mission

To inspire and equip youth across Namibia with boxing equipment – gloves, head gear, punching bags, and more. Our goal is to use boxing as a tool to fight poverty, build discipline, and empower the next generation of champions.

Vision

To launch the Gloves Warrior Sparring Club and Gym for all weight classes, and promote Namibian and African talent to compete internationally for world titles.
